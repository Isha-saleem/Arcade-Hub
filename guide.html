WaveCore Game Guide

Learn how to master the forces of WaveCore!

1. Time Wave

**Concept:** Time Manipulation
**How to Play:** In Time Wave, you'll encounter various events and obstacles. Your primary ability is to control the flow of time:

  • **Slow:** Reduce the speed of all in-game actions to react more precisely.
  • **Pause:** Temporarily freeze the game world to plan your next move or avoid immediate danger.
  • **Rewind:** Reverse recent events to correct mistakes or undo unfavorable outcomes.
The AI will predict your moves based on your time manipulation, so use your abilities strategically to outsmart it and achieve your objectives.

2. Sound Wave

**Concept:** Rhythm/Reaction
**How to Play:** This game challenges your auditory perception and reaction speed. You will need to:

  • **Hit Correct Beats:** Listen to the rhythm and tap/click at precise moments to score points or activate abilities.
  • **Destroy Enemy Waves via Sound:** Generate specific sound patterns or frequencies to counteract incoming enemy waves.
The AI will react to your rhythm, mimic patterns, or try to disrupt your flow. Stay in sync and use sound as your weapon!

3. Heat Wave

**Concept:** Survival
**How to Play:** Survive in an environment with constantly rising temperatures. Your goal is to:

  • **Avoid Rising Temperatures:** Navigate the environment to stay in cooler zones or minimize exposure to heat.
  • **Find Water/Shelter:** Locate and utilize resources like water sources to cool down or shelters to protect yourself from the scorching heat.
The AI will actively try to block your paths to resources or compete with you for the limited safe zones, making survival a strategic challenge.

4. Memory Wave

**Concept:** Pattern Recall
**How to Play:** Test your memory and cognitive abilities by:

  • **Matching Sequences:** Observe and accurately reproduce increasingly complex visual or auditory sequences.
  • **Retracing Memory Paths:** Navigate mazes or environments by remembering previously revealed paths or patterns.
The AI will attempt to confuse you, subtly shift correct sequences, or introduce distractions to challenge your recall. Pay close attention to every detail!

5. Emotion Wave

**Concept:** Mood Control
**How to Play:** This game is about emotional intelligence and balance. You will need to:

  • **Maintain Inner Balance:** Keep your character's emotional state within a desired range, reacting to in-game events that try to sway your mood.
  • **Manipulate Othersâ€™ Moods:** Influence the emotional states of NPCs or even the AI itself to achieve game objectives.
The AI's emotion shifts will directly affect game logic, changing obstacles, opportunities, or even its own behavior based on its current mood.

6. Gravity Wave

**Concept:** Gravitational Manipulation
**How to Play:** Control the forces of gravity to your advantage:

  • **Alter Gravity:** Increase or decrease the gravitational pull on your character or specific objects.
  • **Create Gravity Wells/Fields:** Generate localized gravitational effects to attract or repel objects, solve puzzles, or navigate complex terrain.
The AI will predict your movements under varying gravity, set up gravitational traps, or use gravitational fields to interfere with your progress, challenging your understanding of physics.
